# Random sample of 68 action and romance movies

A random sample of 32 action movies and 36 romance movies from
<https://www.imdb.com/> and their ratings.

## Usage

``` r
movies_sample
```

## Format

A data frame of 68 rows movies.

- title:

  Movie title

- year:

  Year released

- rating:

  IMDb rating out of 10 stars

- genre:

  Action or Romance

## See also

This data was sampled from the `movies` data frame in the
`ggplot2movies` package.
